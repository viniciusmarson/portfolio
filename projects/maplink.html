Principal outcome

Developed the "Excluded Zone" feature, a powerful tool that allows users to define specific areas on the map where routes are strictly prohibited from passing. This feature empowers users to establish non-navigable zones, enhancing control and customization in route planning. By simply designating restricted regions, users can ensure that generated routes will automatically avoid these areas, which is especially useful for applications in logistics, urban planning, and service delivery.

This feature not only added significant flexibility to the platform but also distinguished the company from competitors. It addressed real-world needs, such as avoiding high-traffic zones, hazardous areas, or restricted regions, ultimately optimizing route efficiency and safety for users.
