About the company

Based in São Paulo, Brazil, Singu (now Bluma) is a platform to provide beauty and well-being services wherever you are, on your own time.

Responsibilities

Led a cross-functional team to implement scalable solutions that improved platform performance and stability for the marketplace, handling high traffic during peak loads.

Optimized backend architecture using Serverless Architecture, resulting in increased system reliability and cost savings on infrastructure.

Spearheaded the migration from native apps to React Native, reducing payroll costs by 30% and cutting delivery timelines by consolidating efforts into two codebases instead of managing four native apps.

Principal outcome

Led Singu’s technical evolution over nearly six years — from a 7-person seed-stage startup to the largest beauty and wellness marketplace in Latin America. Built a scalable platform from the ground up, grew the engineering team from 7 to 40+ engineers, and drove the product strategy that made the company an attractive acquisition target for Natura & Co (owner of The Body Shop), one of the world’s largest beauty conglomerates.
